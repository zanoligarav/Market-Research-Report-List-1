<p><strong>The Acoustic Biosensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Quartz Crystal Microbalance (QCM) Sensor</li><li>Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Acoustic Biosensors market includes Quartz Crystal Microbalance (QCM) and Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) sensors. QCM sensors operate by measuring changes in frequency as mass binds to the crystal surface, offering high sensitivity for detecting biomolecular interactions. SAW sensors utilize surface acoustic waves to detect variations in mass or mechanical properties, providing fast response times and the ability to operate in various environments. Both types are instrumental in applications like medical diagnostics, environmental monitoring, and food safety testing.</p></p>
