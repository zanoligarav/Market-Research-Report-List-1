<p><p>Repair shop software can be categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises. On-premises software, on the other hand, is installed locally on a shop's hardware, offering greater control over data and security but requiring more upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. Both types cater to different business needs and operational preferences.</p></p>
