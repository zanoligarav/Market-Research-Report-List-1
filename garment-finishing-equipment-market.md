<p><p>The garment finishing equipment market encompasses various types of machinery designed to enhance the appearance and quality of textiles. Tunnel finishers use steam and heat to remove wrinkles from bulk garments. Presses apply pressure and heat for precise shaping and finishing. Ironer tables offer flat surfaces for manual pressing. Rail systems facilitate the movement of garments throughout the finishing process, while sortation systems efficiently categorize items for further processing. Folders automate folding, ensuring consistent and neat presentation of finished garments.</p></p>
