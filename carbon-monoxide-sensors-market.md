<p><strong>The Carbon Monoxide Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Semiconductor</li><li>Infrared</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carbon monoxide sensors market consists of two primary types: semiconductor and infrared sensors. Semiconductor sensors detect CO levels by measuring changes in electrical resistance when exposed to gas, offering cost-effectiveness and compact design, making them popular for residential and commercial applications. In contrast, infrared sensors operate by detecting specific wavelengths of infrared light absorbed by CO molecules, ensuring high accuracy and reliability, particularly in industrial settings. Both types play crucial roles in safety and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
