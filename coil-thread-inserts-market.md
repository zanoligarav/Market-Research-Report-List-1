<p><p>Coil thread inserts are specialized fasteners used to repair or reinforce threaded holes in various materials. The market is primarily divided into two types: tanged thread inserts and tangless thread inserts. Tanged thread inserts feature tangs for ease of installation and removal, offering strong anchoring capabilities. In contrast, tangless thread inserts provide a smooth installation process, making them ideal for applications requiring minimal disruption to the base material. Both types serve distinct needs in industries ranging from automotive to aerospace.</p></p>
