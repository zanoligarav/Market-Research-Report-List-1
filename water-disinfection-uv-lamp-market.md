<p><p>The water disinfection UV lamp market consists of three main types: low pressure, medium pressure, and high pressure. Low pressure UV lamps emit a single wavelength, making them highly effective for microbial disinfection in smaller applications. Medium pressure lamps offer a broader spectrum, allowing for faster treatment of larger volumes but are less efficient per watt. High pressure UV lamps are designed for industrial and municipal water treatment, providing high intensity and effectiveness over extensive systems. Each type serves distinct disinfection needs based on application scale and efficiency.</p></p>
