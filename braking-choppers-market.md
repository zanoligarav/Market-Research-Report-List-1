In contrast, regenerative braking choppers capture and redirect energy back into the power supply or battery, enhancing efficiency and energy recovery. Both types support electric vehicles and train systems, contributing to improved performance, energy management, and reduced wear on braking components, thus extending their operational lifespan.</p></p>
