<p><p>The printing machine market comprises several key types: web offset lithography, flexography, gravure, and others. Web offset lithography is ideal for high-speed production of newspapers and magazines, utilizing continuous rolls of paper. Flexography excels at printing on various substrates, including packaging materials, thanks to its quick-drying inks. Gravure provides high-quality images for packaging and publication through engraved cylinders. Other printing technologies include digital and screen printing, each catering to specific applications and market needs.</p></p>
