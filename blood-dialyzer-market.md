<p><p>The blood dialyzer market consists of two primary types: reusable and disposable blood dialyzers. Reusable blood dialyzers are designed for multiple uses, typically requiring sterilization between treatments, which can reduce costs over time. In contrast, disposable blood dialyzers are intended for single-use, providing convenience and minimizing the risk of infection. Both types serve the critical role of filtering waste and toxins from the blood in patients with renal failure, ensuring effective dialysis treatment.</p></p>
