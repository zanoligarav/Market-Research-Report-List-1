<p><strong>The Ballast Water Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chemical Method</li><li>Physical Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ballast water market is divided into two main types: chemical and physical methods. Chemical methods involve the use of biocides to neutralize invasive species in ballast water, ensuring compliance with maritime regulations. In contrast, physical methods rely on techniques such as filtration, UV radiation, and heat treatment to remove or kill organisms without chemicals. Both approaches aim to prevent the introduction of harmful aquatic species into new environments, safeguarding marine ecosystems while meeting international standards for shipping.</p></p>
