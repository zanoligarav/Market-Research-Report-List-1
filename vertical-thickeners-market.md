<p><ul><li>Vertical Gravity Thickeners</li><li>Vertical Screw Thickeners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vertical thickeners market includes two primary types: vertical gravity thickeners and vertical screw thickeners. Vertical gravity thickeners utilize gravity to separate solids from liquids, resulting in a concentrated sludge and clarified effluent. They are efficient for wastewater treatment. In contrast, vertical screw thickeners employ a screw mechanism to transport and dewater sludge, enhancing the concentration process. Both types are essential in various industries, including municipal wastewater treatment and industrial processes, contributing to improved solid-liquid separation and resource recovery.</p></p>
