Additionally, the growing acceptance of dental implants as a standard treatment for edentulous patients contributes to market expansion. The increasing focus on aesthetic considerations in dentistry further fuels demand for innovative solutions in this sector, as patients seek options that not only restore functionality but also enhance their smile.
