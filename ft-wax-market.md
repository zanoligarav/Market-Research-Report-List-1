<p><p>FT Wax is categorized into three market types based on chain length. The C35-C80 type consists of relatively low molecular weight waxes, used primarily in cosmetics and packaging due to their flexibility and barrier properties. The C80-C100 type features higher molecular weight waxes that offer enhanced stability and performance for applications like coatings and inks. Lastly, the C100+ type encompasses the highest molecular weight waxes, utilized in industrial applications requiring superior durability and resistance. Each type serves distinct industrial needs.</p></p>
