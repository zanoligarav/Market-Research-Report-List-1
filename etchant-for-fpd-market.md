<p><p>The etchant for the Flat Panel Display (FPD) market comprises two main types: metal etchants and non-metal etchants. Metal etchants are primarily used for patterning conductive materials like copper and aluminum in display circuits, ensuring precise feature definition. Non-metal etchants, on the other hand, target dielectric materials such as silicon oxide and silicon nitride, facilitating the creation of insulating layers. Both types are crucial for manufacturing high-performance displays, optimizing efficiency and enhancing the overall display quality.</p></p>
