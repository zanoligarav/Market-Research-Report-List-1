<p><p>Rubber seated butterfly valves are essential components in various fluid control applications, available in two primary types: wafer and lug. Wafer type valves feature a compact design and are installed between two flanges, making them ideal for tight spaces and cost-effective solutions. In contrast, lug type valves are equipped with threaded holes on their body, allowing for easy installation and removal without disrupting the pipeline. Both types ensure reliable sealing and flow control, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
