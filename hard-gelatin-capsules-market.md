<p><p>Hard gelatin capsules are solid dosage forms made from gelatin, primarily used for encapsulating powdered or granulated substances. They consist of two parts: a body and a cap, allowing for easy filling and administration. These capsules are preferred in the pharmaceutical and nutraceutical industries due to their ability to mask taste, provide a barrier against moisture, and enhance bioavailability of drugs.</p><p>The Hard Gelatin Capsules Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.2% during the forecast period. Bovine capsules are made from cattle gelatin, providing a desirable alternative for certain dietary preferences. Bone meal capsules utilize gelatin derived from animal bones, positioned as a more sustainable option.
