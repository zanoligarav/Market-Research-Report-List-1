<p><p>The Linear Variable Displacement Transducer (LVDT) market is primarily divided into AC LVDTs and DC LVDTs. AC LVDTs, powered by an alternating current, offer high accuracy and are commonly used in industrial automation and aerospace applications due to their robustness and reliability. In contrast, DC LVDTs utilize direct current, providing simplified integration with digital systems. They are often employed in medical and automotive applications where compactness and ease of use are essential. Both types cater to diverse sectors, driven by technological advancements.</p></p>
