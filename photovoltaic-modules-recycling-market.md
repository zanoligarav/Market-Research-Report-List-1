<p><p>The photovoltaic modules recycling market consists of three main types: monocrystalline, polycrystalline, and thin-film modules. Monocrystalline modules, known for their high efficiency and durability, require specialized recycling processes due to their silicon composition. Polycrystalline modules, which are more cost-effective and less efficient, have simpler recycling approaches but still involve silicon recovery. Thin-film modules, made from layers of semiconductor materials, present unique recycling challenges due to their diverse materials. Each type demands tailored recycling solutions to recover valuable components and minimize environmental impact.</p></p>
