<p><ul><li>E Type</li><li>F Type</li><li>N Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Microflute market types—E, F, and N—refer to different flute profiles used in corrugated packaging. **E Type** features fine flutes, offering excellent printability and surface smoothness, ideal for retail packaging. **F Type** has slightly larger flutes, balancing strength and print quality, often used for lightweight yet durable packaging. **N Type** has the smallest flutes, providing superior rigidity and compression strength, suitable for heavy-duty applications. Each type caters to specific needs, balancing strength, printability, and material efficiency in packaging solutions.</p></p>
