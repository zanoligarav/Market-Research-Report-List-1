<p><p>The accelerometer sensor board market is categorized into three primary types: Resistance, Capacitive, and Voltage accelerometer sensor boards. Resistance accelerometers measure changes in resistance due to acceleration, while capacitive accelerometers utilize changes in capacitance for detection, offering high sensitivity and stability. Voltage accelerometers convert acceleration into a voltage signal, enabling straightforward data interpretation. Each type serves distinct applications across industries, such as automotive, consumer electronics, and aerospace, allowing for precise motion detection and monitoring.</p></p>
