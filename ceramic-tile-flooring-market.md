<p><ul><li>Glazed</li><li>Unglazed</li><li>Lapped</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ceramic tile flooring is categorized primarily into three types: glazed, unglazed, and lapped tiles. Glazed tiles have a smooth, colorful surface layer that provides durability and moisture resistance, making them ideal for high-traffic areas. Unglazed tiles retain a natural look and offer better slip resistance but require sealing for stain protection. Lapped tiles, often featuring a textured surface, provide an aesthetic appeal that mimics natural materials while enhancing grip, making them suitable for both indoor and outdoor applications.</p></p>
