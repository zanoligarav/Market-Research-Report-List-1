<p><p>The nanomanipulator market primarily includes two key types: Electron Microscopes (EM) and Scanning Probe Microscopes (SPM). Electron Microscopes utilize electron beams to achieve high-resolution imaging of nanostructures, enabling precise manipulation at the atomic level. In contrast, Scanning Probe Microscopes employ various probes to measure surface properties and manipulate materials with exceptional accuracy. Both technologies are crucial in advancing nanotechnology research and development, offering diverse applications in materials science, biology, and semiconductor industries.</p></p>
