<p><strong>The Epoxy Laminating Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Unfilled</li><li>Filled</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The epoxy laminating resin market is categorized into unfilled and filled types. Unfilled epoxy resin contains no additives, offering superior clarity and excellent adhesion, making it ideal for applications requiring transparency, such as coatings and adhesives. In contrast, filled epoxy resin includes materials like silica or calcium carbonate, enhancing properties such as thermal stability and mechanical strength. This makes filled resins suitable for more demanding applications, including automotive, aerospace, and civil engineering, where enhanced performance is crucial.</p></p>
