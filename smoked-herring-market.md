<p><strong>The Smoked Herring Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot-smoke</li><li>Cold-smoke</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smoked herring market is divided into two primary types: hot-smoked and cold-smoked. Hot-smoked herring is cooked and smoked simultaneously at higher temperatures, resulting in a firmer texture and distinct flavor, making it popular for immediate consumption or as a cooking ingredient. In contrast, cold-smoked herring is cured and then smoked at lower temperatures, creating a more delicate texture and subtler taste, ideal for garnishes or specialty dishes. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and culinary uses.</p></p>
