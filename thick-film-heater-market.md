<p><p>The thick film heater market is categorized into metal and ceramic thick film heaters. Metal thick film heaters utilize conductive metal traces on a substrate, offering efficient heat distribution and durability, making them ideal for industrial applications. In contrast, ceramic thick film heaters use ceramic materials for insulation, providing excellent thermal stability and resistance to high temperatures, which suits applications in electronics and medical devices. Both types leverage the advantages of thick film technology for precise heating solutions across various sectors.</p></p>
