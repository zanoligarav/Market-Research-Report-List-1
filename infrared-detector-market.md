<p><strong>The Infrared Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Uncooled Infrared Detector</li><li>Cooled Infrared Detector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infrared detector market comprises two main types: uncooled and cooled infrared detectors. Uncooled infrared detectors function at ambient temperatures, making them cost-effective and suitable for portable applications, such as thermal imaging cameras and consumer electronics. In contrast, cooled infrared detectors require cooling mechanisms to enhance sensitivity and detect lower levels of infrared radiation, making them ideal for high-performance applications like military, aerospace, and industrial surveillance. Both types play crucial roles in advancing imaging technologies across various sectors.</p></p>
