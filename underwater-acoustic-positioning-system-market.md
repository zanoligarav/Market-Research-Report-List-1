<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Underwater Acoustic Positioning System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ultra-short Baseline(USBL)</li><li>Short Baseline(SBL)</li><li>Long Baseline(LBL)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Underwater Acoustic Positioning System market comprises three main types: Ultra-short Baseline (USBL), Short Baseline (SBL), and Long Baseline (LBL). USBL systems utilize a single transducer to determine the position of underwater objects or vehicles by measuring the time taken for sound waves to travel. SBL uses multiple hydrophones in a short configuration for enhanced accuracy. LBL relies on a network of fixed transponders on the seabed for precise positioning over longer distances, making it suitable for complex underwater operations.</p></p>
