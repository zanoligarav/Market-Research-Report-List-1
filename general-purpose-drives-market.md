<p><p>General Purpose Drives are crucial components in industrial automation, categorized into low voltage and high voltage drives. Low Voltage General Purpose Drives typically operate below 1,000 volts, controlling smaller motors in applications like HVAC, pumps, and conveyors. High Voltage General Purpose Drives, on the other hand, manage larger motors, often exceeding 1,000 volts, and are used in heavy industries such as mining and manufacturing. Both types enhance energy efficiency, improve motor control, and support various operational demands in diverse sectors.</p></p>
