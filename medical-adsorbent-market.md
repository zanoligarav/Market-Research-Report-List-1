Synthetic resins are utilized for their stability and selective binding capabilities, often applied in drug delivery systems. Activated carbon is favored for its high surface area and ability to adsorb toxins and impurities, commonly used in detoxification processes. Polysaccharides, derived from natural sources, offer biocompatibility and are employed in various medical applications, including wound healing and drug formulation, enhancing therapeutic efficacy.</p></p>
