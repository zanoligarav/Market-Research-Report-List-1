<p><p>The spacer tape market is primarily categorized into single-sided adhesive and double-sided adhesive tapes. Single-sided adhesive tapes feature adhesive on one side, making them ideal for applications where only one surface requires bonding, providing a clean appearance and ease of use. In contrast, double-sided tapes have adhesive on both sides, allowing for better adhesion between two surfaces, making them suitable for various applications like mounting, insulation, and assembling components. Both types serve essential roles in diverse industries, enhancing productivity and efficiency.</p></p>
