<p><p>The closed impeller pump market can be categorized into multi-stage and single-stage pumps. Single-stage pumps feature one impeller, making them ideal for low-pressure applications, while multi-stage pumps consist of multiple impellers that increase pressure, suitable for high-pressure needs. Multi-stage pumps are commonly used in applications like water treatment and oil refining, whereas single-stage pumps are prevalent in residential and light industrial uses. Each type serves specific operational requirements based on flow and pressure conditions.</p></p>
