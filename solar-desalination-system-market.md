<p><p>The solar desalination system market encompasses several technologies for converting seawater into fresh water. Direct distillation systems utilize solar energy to heat water, causing evaporation and subsequent condensation, producing potable water. Reverse osmosis systems use solar power to drive high-pressure pumps that force seawater through semi-permeable membranes, separating salt and impurities. Other methods include solar stills, solar-powered electrodialysis, and hybrid systems. Each technology varies in efficiency, scalability, and application, catering to diverse needs and geographical settings in water-scarce regions.</p></p>
