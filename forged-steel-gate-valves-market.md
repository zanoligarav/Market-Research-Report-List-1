<p><p>The forged steel gate valves market is categorized into three main types based on their bonnet design: bolted bonnet, welded bonnet, and pressure seal bonnet. Bolted bonnet valves are accessible for maintenance due to their removable design. Welded bonnet valves feature a permanent seal, enhancing integrity and reducing leakage risk, making them suitable for high-pressure applications. Pressure seal bonnet valves employ a unique sealing mechanism that maintains a tight seal under pressure, making them ideal for critical high-pressure settings. Each type serves specific operational needs and industry requirements.</p></p>
