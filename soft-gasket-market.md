<p><p>The soft gasket market is segmented into two main types: semi-metallic and non-metallic materials. Semi-metallic gaskets, often composed of materials like graphite or rubber combined with metal, provide enhanced strength and resilience, making them suitable for high-temperature and high-pressure applications. In contrast, non-metallic gaskets, made from materials such as rubber, PTFE, or compressed fiber, offer excellent sealing properties and are ideal for less demanding conditions. Both types serve critical roles in ensuring leak-proof seals across various industries.</p></p>
