<p><p>The 3D printed polymers market encompasses various technologies, notably stereolithography (SLA) and fused deposition modeling (FDM). SLA utilizes laser light to cure liquid resin into solid structures, enabling high-resolution and intricate designs, ideal for prototypes and intricate models. FDM, on the other hand, extrudes thermoplastic filaments layer by layer, making it accessible and widely used for functional parts and educational purposes. Both methods cater to diverse applications, from aerospace to healthcare, contributing to the growing adoption of 3D printing technology.</p></p>
