<p><p>The laboratory diagnostics market comprises several key segments. Drug testing analyzes substances in biological samples to ensure compliance or detect abuse. Clinical chemistry analysis evaluates blood and other fluids to assess organ function and diseases. Immunoassay utilizes antibodies for detecting specific substances, often hormones or infectious agents. Hematology analysis examines blood components to diagnose disorders. Urinalysis assesses urine for medical conditions. Molecular diagnostics focuses on genetic material for precise disease identification. The "Others" category encompasses various specialized tests and emerging technologies.</p></p>
