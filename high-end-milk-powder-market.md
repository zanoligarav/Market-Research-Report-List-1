<p><p>The high-end milk powder market includes two primary types: whole milk powder and skim milk powder. Whole milk powder contains all the fat naturally found in milk, offering a creamy texture and rich flavor, making it ideal for premium products like desserts and sauces. Skim milk powder, on the other hand, has the fat removed, resulting in a lighter consistency and lower calorie content. This type is often used in health-conscious formulations and products targeting fitness enthusiasts. Both types cater to gourmet and specialized markets.</p></p>
