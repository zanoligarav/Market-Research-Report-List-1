<p><strong>The Ultrasonic Flow Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inline Type</li><li>Clamp-On Type</li><li>Insertion Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultrasonic flow meter market comprises three primary types: inline, clamp-on, and insertion. Inline type flow meters are installed directly within the pipeline, offering high accuracy and direct measurement of fluid flow. Clamp-on type meters are positioned externally, using ultrasonic signals to measure flow without disrupting the pipeline, making them ideal for retrofit applications. Insertion type flow meters penetrate into the fluid stream, balancing the benefits of inline meters with ease of installation, suitable for various industrial applications.</p></p>
