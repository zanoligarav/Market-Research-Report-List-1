<p><p>The Automotive Millimeter-Wave Radar IC market encompasses three primary technology types: GaAs, SiGe BiCMOS, and RF CMOS. GaAs (Gallium Arsenide) offers high-frequency performance and efficiency, ideal for advanced radar systems. SiGe BiCMOS combines the benefits of silicon and germanium, providing cost-effectiveness while maintaining strong performance for moderate range applications. RF CMOS (Radio Frequency Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) integrates digital and analog functions, enabling compact designs and lower power consumption, making it suitable for various automotive radar solutions.</p></p>
