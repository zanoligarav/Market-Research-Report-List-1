<p><p>Magnesium Hydroxide Paste is produced primarily through two methods: chemical synthesis and physical methods. The chemical synthesis method involves the reaction of magnesium salts with bases, resulting in a consistent and high-purity product. In contrast, the physical method focuses on mechanical processes, such as milling or mixing, to create the paste without altering the chemical composition. Each method affects the paste's texture, viscosity, and application suitability, catering to diverse industries like pharmaceuticals, personal care, and environmental applications.</p></p>
