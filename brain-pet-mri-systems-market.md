<p><p>The Brain PET-MRI Systems market is segmented into two main types: Traditional PET-MRI Systems and Helium-free PET-MRI Systems. Traditional systems utilize liquid helium for cooling the MRI magnets, ensuring high-quality imaging but often facing supply constraints. In contrast, Helium-free PET-MRI Systems eliminate the reliance on helium, employing alternative cooling technologies, which enhances sustainability and reduces operational costs. Both types serve critical roles in neuroimaging, aiding in the diagnosis and monitoring of neurological conditions through combined metabolic and structural data.</p></p>
