<p><strong>The Onsite Nitrogen Generation Units Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Membrane Nitrogen Generators</li><li>PSA Nitrogen Generators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Onsite Nitrogen Generation Units are categorized mainly into two types: Membrane Nitrogen Generators and PSA Nitrogen Generators. Membrane generators use a selective permeable membrane to separate nitrogen from compressed air, providing a continuous flow of nitrogen suitable for applications like packaging and food preservation. In contrast, PSA (Pressure Swing Adsorption) generators utilize adsorbent materials to capture nitrogen from air under pressure, allowing for high purity levels and efficiency, making them ideal for industrial applications like chemical processing and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
