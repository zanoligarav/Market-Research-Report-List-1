The market comprises two main types: Vane Air Starters, which utilize rotating vanes to generate torque for engine ignition, and Turbine Air Starters, which operate using high-speed air jets to drive a turbine. Vane air starters offer simplicity and compact design, while turbine air starters provide high power and efficiency, making both types vital for optimizing engine performance in demanding environments.</p></p>
