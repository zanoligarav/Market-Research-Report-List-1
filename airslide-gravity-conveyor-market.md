<p><ul><li>Fluidized Gravity Systems</li><li>Positive Pressure Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Airslide Gravity Conveyor market encompasses two primary types: fluidized gravity systems and positive pressure systems. Fluidized gravity systems utilize air to create a bed of air, allowing materials to glide smoothly along a sloped surface. In contrast, positive pressure systems employ an influx of air to propel materials gently, maintaining speed and reducing friction. Both systems enhance material handling efficiency in various industries, offering flexibility in design and operation while minimizing wear and energy consumption.</p></p>
