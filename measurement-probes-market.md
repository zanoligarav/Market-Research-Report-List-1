<p><ul><li>Contact Probe</li><li>Non Contact Probe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Measurement probes are essential tools used in various industries for precise data acquisition. The market is divided into two main types: contact and non-contact probes. Contact probes physically touch the test surface to gather measurements, offering high accuracy for tasks like dimensional inspection. In contrast, non-contact probes utilize technologies such as lasers or ultrasonic waves to measure without direct contact, making them ideal for delicate or moving objects. Both types cater to specific application needs, enhancing operational efficiency and accuracy.</p></p>
