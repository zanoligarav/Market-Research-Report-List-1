<p><p>The video router market can be divided into two main types: analog and digital video formats. Analog video formats utilize continuous signals to transmit video, typically offering lower resolution and quality but requiring simpler technology. In contrast, digital video formats convert video signals into data, enabling higher resolutions and better image quality, along with advanced features like compression and streaming capabilities. As technology advances, digital formats are increasingly dominating the market due to their scalability and efficiency in handling various content types.</p></p>
