<p><p>Passenger Emergency Oxygen Deployment Systems are essential for ensuring passenger safety during in-flight emergencies. The market primarily includes two types: Chemical Oxygen Generators and Compressed Oxygen Systems. Chemical Oxygen Generators produce oxygen through a chemical reaction, providing a reliable and lightweight solution. They activate automatically in emergencies, delivering oxygen to passengers quickly. In contrast, Compressed Oxygen Systems store oxygen in high-pressure cylinders, allowing for immediate access. Both systems are designed to support passenger needs during sudden loss of cabin pressure, enhancing overall flight safety.</p></p>
