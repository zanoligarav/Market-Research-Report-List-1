<p><strong>The Online Process Turbidimeter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transmission</li><li>Scattering</li><li>Scattering-Transmission</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The online process turbidimeter market comprises three main types: transmission, scattering, and scattering-transmission. Transmission turbidimeters assess the clarity of liquids by measuring light that passes through a sample, making them suitable for clean water applications. Scattering turbidimeters detect light scattered by particles in a sample, ideal for wastewater and industrial processes with higher turbidity levels. Scattering-transmission turbidimeters combine both methods, allowing for comprehensive analysis across varying turbidity conditions, enhancing accuracy in water quality monitoring.</p></p>
