<p><p>Incentive cards, used to motivate consumer behavior, can be classified by functional attributes into open-loop and closed-loop systems. Open-loop cards allow use across multiple merchants, while closed-loop cards are restricted to specific retailers. By product type, they can be categorized into registered cards, which require user identification and can track spending, and bearer cards, offering anonymity. Each type serves distinct purposes, from loyalty programs to promotional incentives, enhancing customer engagement and transactional efficiency.</p></p>
