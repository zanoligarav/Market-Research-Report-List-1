<p><strong>The Smart Sorting Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear Sortation Systems</li><li>Loop Sortation Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Smart Sorting Machine market includes two primary types: Linear Sortation Systems and Loop Sortation Systems. Linear Sortation Systems operate in a straight line configuration, conveying items through a series of chutes or gates to direct them to specific destinations, optimizing space and minimizing congestion. In contrast, Loop Sortation Systems use a continuous loop layout, allowing items to be sorted efficiently in a circular motion, enhancing throughput and flexibility in handling diverse product types.</p></p>
