<p><p>The Mobile Automatic Number Plate Recognition (ANPR) Systems market comprises several key components. ANPR cameras capture high-resolution images of vehicle license plates on-the-go, while ANPR software processes and interprets this data for various applications, such as law enforcement and parking management. Frame grabbers act as intermediaries, facilitating the transfer of images from cameras to software. Triggers, such as sensors or GPS, initiate the data capture process, ensuring timely recognition and enhancing system efficiency across mobile deployments.</p></p>
