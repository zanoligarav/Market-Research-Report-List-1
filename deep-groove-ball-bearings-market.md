<p><p>The deep-groove ball bearings market consists of three main types: single-row, double-row, and multi-row bearings. Single-row bearings are widely used for their simplicity and versatility, providing effective radial and axial load support. Double-row bearings offer enhanced load capacity and stability, suitable for heavy-duty applications. Each type caters to different operational requirements across various industries.</p></p>
