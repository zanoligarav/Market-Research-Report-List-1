<p><ul><li>Soft Contact Lenses</li><li>Rigid Gas Permeable (RGP) Contact Lenses</li><li>Hybrid Contact Lenses</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The corrective contact lenses market is segmented into three main types: soft contact lenses, rigid gas permeable (RGP) lenses, and hybrid lenses. Soft lenses offer comfort and flexibility, making them popular for daily wear. RGP lenses provide sharper vision and are more durable, ideal for those with specific vision needs. Hybrid lenses combine the benefits of both, featuring a gas-permeable center for clarity with a soft skirt for enhanced comfort. Each type caters to different user preferences and vision correction requirements.</p></p>
