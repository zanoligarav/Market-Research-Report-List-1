<p><p>The Printed Circuit Board (PCB) technologies market encompasses three main types: single-sided, double-sided, and multi-layer boards. Single-sided PCBs feature components on one side, making them cost-effective and simple, suitable for low-density applications. Double-sided PCBs have components on both sides, allowing for increased complexity and more connections, ideal for various electronic devices. Multi-layer PCBs consist of multiple layers of circuits, enabling high-density interconnections and advanced functionality, essential for sophisticated electronics in telecommunications, computing, and automotive industries.</p></p>
