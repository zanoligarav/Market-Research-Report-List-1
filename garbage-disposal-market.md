<p><p>The garbage disposal market primarily consists of two types: batch feed and continuous feed. Batch feed disposals require users to close a lid before operation, allowing them to add food waste in controlled amounts, enhancing safety. In contrast, continuous feed models operate continuously when switched on, enabling users to add waste without interruption. Both types cater to different consumer needs, with batch feed being ideal for safety-conscious users and continuous feed preferred for those seeking convenience and efficiency in waste disposal.</p></p>
