<p><p>The interbody devices market encompasses three main categories: instruments, implants, and supporting devices. Instruments include surgical tools used for insertion and manipulation during spinal procedures. Implants are the core devices placed between vertebrae to facilitate bone fusion and maintain spinal stability. Supporting devices consist of adjunctive items, such as spacer trays or fixation mechanisms, that enhance the effectiveness of implants and instruments. Together, these categories contribute to advancements in spinal surgeries and improved patient outcomes.</p></p>
